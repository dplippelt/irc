STARTING & MANAGING GAMES

!start
  Start playing a single player battlehips game.

!newgame
  Start a fresh single player game (will abort your currently running game).

!challenge <username>
  Challenge another user to a game of battleships (e.g. '!challenge bob). Please make sure to enter the username correctly or they will not receive the challenge!

!accept <username>
  Accept another user's challenge to a game of battleships (e.g. '!accept alice).

!surrender <username>
  Surrender to another player. This will immediately end your game with them (e.g. '!surrender bob' to surrender to bob).


ATTACKING

!fire <target>
  Fire a shot at the board in single player (e.g. '!fire B3').

!shoot <username> <target>
  Take a shot at another user's board (e.g. '!shoot bob H4')


INFORMATION COMMANDS

!board
  Show your current game board in single player.

!fleet <username>
  Show your current fleet against another player (e.g. '!fleet bob'). Will be visible to only you.

!shots <username>
  Show your current shots grid against another player (e.g. '!shots bob'). Will be visible to only you.

!solution
  Show the single player board with all ships visible (i.e. cheat sheet).

!files
  List available files for download.

!file <filename>
  Download a specific file (e.g. '!file rules.txt').

!help
  Display available commands
