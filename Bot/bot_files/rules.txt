═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                               OBJECTIVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours! Each player has
a fleet of ships placed secretly on their grid. Take turns calling out
coordinates to attack. The first player to destroy all enemy ships wins!


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              GAME MODES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SINGLE PLAYER MODE
  • Play against the bot
  • Command: !start
  • Practice your skills and learn strategies

MULTIPLAYER MODE
  • Challenge other users on the server
  • Command: !challenge <username>
  • Accept challenges: !accept <username>
  • Competitive gameplay with friends


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                               THE FLEET
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Each player controls a fleet of 5 ships:

  Ship Name        | Symbol | Length
  ─────────────────┼────────┼────────
  Carrier          |   K    |   5
  Battleship       |   B    |   4
  Cruiser          |   C    |   3
  Submarine        |   S    |   3
  Destroyer        |   D    |   2


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                             THE GAME GRID
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The ocean grid is 8x8, labeled with:
  • Columns: 1 through 8 (horizontal)
  • Rows: A through H (vertical)

Example grid positions:

        A
        B
        C
        D
        E
        F
        G
        H
12345678

Legend:
     ' '    = Water (unexplored)
      0     = Miss (you've shot here, but hit nothing)
      X     = Hit (you've damaged a ship)
  K/B/C/S/D = Sunk ship


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                            SHIP PLACEMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

AUTOMATIC PLACEMENT
  • Ships are placed randomly on your grid at game start
  • Ships can be horizontal or vertical
  • Ships cannot overlap
  • Ships cannot extend beyond the grid boundaries

PLACEMENT RULES
  • Each ship occupies consecutive squares
  • Ships must be placed in a straight line
  • No diagonal placement
  • Ships can touch, but cannot overlap


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                               GAMEPLAY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TAKING YOUR TURN

Single Player:
  !fire <target>
  Example: !fire B5

Multiplayer:
  !shoot <username> <target>
  Example: !shoot alice C7


SHOT RESULTS

HIT (X)
  • You've struck part of an enemy ship
  • Your turn ends
  • Opponent's turn begins

MISS (0)
  • Your shot hit only water
  • Your turn ends
  • Opponent's turn begins

SUNK
  • All squares of a ship have been hit
  • The ship is destroyed and the HIT markers (X) are replaced with the ship's symbols (K/B/C/S/D)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                           COORDINATE SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Valid Coordinates:
  • Letter (A-H) + Number (1-8)
  • Letter must come first
  • Case insensitive: "a1" = "A1"

Examples of Valid Shots:
  ✓ A1, A8, H1, J8
  ✓ B5, D7, H3, E6
  ✓ a1, c4, g8 (lowercase works too)

Examples of Invalid Shots:
  ✗ 1A (number first - incorrect order)
  ✗ K5 (column K doesn't exist)
  ✗ B11 (row 11 doesn't exist)
  ✗ BB (no number provided)
  ✗ 55 (no letter provided)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                           WINNING THE GAME
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Victory Condition:
  • Sink ALL 5 of your opponent's ships
  • The first player to destroy the entire enemy fleet wins!

After a ship is sunk:
  • The game announces which ship was destroyed
  • Continue attacking until all ships are eliminated


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                             GAME COMMANDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STARTING & MANAGING GAMES

!start
  Start playing a single player battlehips game.

!newgame
  Start a fresh single player game (will abort your currently running game).

!challenge <username>
  Challenge another user to a game of battleships (e.g. '!challenge bob). Please make sure to enter the username correctly or they will not receive the challenge!

!accept <username>
  Accept another user's challenge to a game of battleships (e.g. '!accept alice).

!surrender <username>
  Surrender to another player. This will immediately end your game with them (e.g. '!surrender bob' to surrender to bob).


ATTACKING

!fire <target>
  Fire a shot at the board in single player (e.g. '!fire B3').

!shoot <username> <target>
  Take a shot at another user's board (e.g. '!shoot bob H4')


INFORMATION COMMANDS

!board
  Show your current game board in single player.

!fleet <username>
  Show your current fleet against another player (e.g. '!fleet bob'). Will be visible to only you.

!shots <username>
  Show your current shots grid against another player (e.g. '!shots bob'). Will be visible to only you.

!solution
  Show the single player board with all ships visible (i.e. cheat sheet).

!files
  List available files for download.

!file <filename>
  Download a specific file (e.g. '!file rules.txt').

!help
  Display available commands


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                         STRATEGY & FAIR PLAY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TIPS FOR BEGINNERS
  • Start by spreading your shots across the grid
  • When you get a hit, try adjacent squares
  • Ships are in straight lines (horizontal or vertical)
  • Larger ships are easier to find but harder to sink

FAIR PLAY RULES
  • No outside communication about ship positions
  • Don't share your grid with opponents
  • Be a good sport - it's just a game!
  • Respect other players
